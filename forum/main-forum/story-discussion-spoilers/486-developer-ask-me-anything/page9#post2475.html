        Originally posted by ChristineMoon View Post
        How do you distinguish mage levels? I assumed it was an emblem on their cloak, but you said Elise can't wear a black cloak anymore, and I admit I'm a little confused.

        Wondering if Coppers wear gray, it would make sense for A.C. to match the color but not the style. What about Bronze?
        Someone's mage level is determined by the emblem on their cloaks, but there are few things to watch out for when you're at Timecrest:

        1) People can have any emblem embroidered on their cloak as long as they have earned that rank.
        2) People can mix and match any emblem with any colored-cloak as long as they have earned that rank.
        3) People don't need to wear their cloaks or emblems, and it might or might not incur penalties.
        4) People are told they need to wear the emblem and cloak that corresponds with their rank.

        Most people tend to wear their highest rank emblem and cloak. And with the exception of some, everyone pretty much loves Timecrest cloaks because they are well-made, help cute muffies and show that they belong to one of the best guilds.

        Disclaimer: This system was set up by Guildmaster Fulgur who said Coppers start out with blue cloaks, and Bronzes can have green cloaks, while Silvers get access to gold cloaks and Golds can start wearing black cloaks. Even though the colors do not optimally correlate with the ranks, they do contrast with the emblem colors so that part makes sense. This system may experience revisions by other Guildmasters someday if they cared to, but most of them are interested in the choices people make. Some say Guildmaster Fulgur is still cackling at this system.
