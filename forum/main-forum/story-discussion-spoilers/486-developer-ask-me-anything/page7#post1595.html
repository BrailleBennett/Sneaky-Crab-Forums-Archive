            Editing a comment
            1) Not going to spoil you on the Valdorian disease. You'll probably hear more about it Timecrest 3. (no promises)
            2) We have a pretty solid story spec'd out so generally player theories aren't changing the storyline. We look at what you guys say and sometimes change text to make things clearer if we felt too many people were misunderstanding something in the story. Or it might mean we add scenes to draw more attention to something.
            3) Valdorians are just Alyncians who live and belong to the Kingdom of Valdor. Humans and Alyncians do look the same. Riley is special, she has magical wings.
            4) Valdor was created by First Mage Ezra 1000 years ago. The Kingdom survived until (nearly) present day, before someone destroyed it.
