    [SPOILERS] List of open mysteries in Timecrest

    11-03-2015, 06:19 PM
    Hi everyone. Just found this forum so I thought I'd post. Loved Timecrest and wanted to make a list of open mysteries that we can use to speculate on what is happening in Timecrest 2.
    • In Chapter 3, Chronos helps a flower girl. Who is she?
    • Why does Chronos want to destroy Valdor/enjoy it so much?
    • What is Irillium used for?
    • "Join me Ash Timecrest" -- does this mean Chronos and Ash are related? (Siblings probably?)
    • Who unfreezes the meteors at the end, and why?
    Please add to this list and we should also talk about how it all might connect together.
