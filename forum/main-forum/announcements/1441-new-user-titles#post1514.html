    03-13-2017, 01:02 PM
    Thanks to South for the idea. We're updating the user title system, so you should see yourself have your new title! Here are the list of titles and the number of posts you need to reach each one:

    User Title Posts Needed
    Muffie 0
    Copper 10
    Bronze 50
    Silver 100
    Gold 300
    Sub-Master 500

    Master Chronos 2,000
    Master Portia 2,500
