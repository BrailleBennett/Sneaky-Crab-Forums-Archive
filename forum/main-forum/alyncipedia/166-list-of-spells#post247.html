    List of Spells

    02-19-2016, 03:14 PM
    I want to start a running list of spells. I want to list what thy do, what item to draw them from and any variants they may have. If you find ones I haven't, feel free to add.

    Name: Gravitas
    Effect: Makes user heavier. Causes user to sink into ground, creating crater
    Spell Item: Pebble

    Name: Gravitas Magnus
    Effect: Makes all surrounding objects heavier. Will cause nearby objects to sink into ground
    Spell Item: None. More powerful variant of Gravitas. Taught by Ezra, the man on Mount Asura

    Name: Gravitas Ultimas
    Effect: Extremely intensifies gravity all around. Used by Chronos to annihilate the Valdorian Army (if you choose Gravitas)
    Spell Item: None.

    Name: Levitas
    Effect: Makes user lighter. Enables floating
    Spell Item: Griffin's egg shell

    Name: Levitas Magnus
    Effect: Makes all surrounding items lighter. Will cause nearby objects to float in air
    Spell Item: None. Stronger variant of Levitas. Taught by Ezra, the man on Moung Asura.

    Name: Levitas Ultimas
    Effect: Throws all objects around user upwards. Used by Chronos to annihilate the Valdorian Army (if you choose Levitas)
    Spell item: None.

    Name: Nexutas
    Effect: Connects Ash's world to yours. Tied into Ash's pocket watch. Translates what you say into language Ash can read and vice versa.
    Spell Item: Apple
    Other Notes: Ash's first spell. Will always have this, no matter what you do

    Name: Unknown. Possibly Teleport
    Effect: Unknown. Likely teleporting user. Possibly companion, as well
    Spell Item: Putportedly shimmering lotus or feather from winged chimera
    Other Notes: If learned, will negate need for Teleport Orbs. One of the spells able to be stored in orbs, creating a Teleport Orb

    Name: Annextas
    Effect: Binds something to an item. In Ash's case, used by Riley to tie money pounch and items pouch to pocket watch, allowing you access, as well as the Aura Amulet.
    Spell Item: Griffin Egg Tome

    Name: Unknown. Possibly Illusion
    Effect: Creates illusions. Fools effected organism through various means. Can create endless tunnels or show disgusted doorways, for example
    Spell Item: Unknown
    Other Notes: Guildmaster Petora's specialty. Can be overcome with Dispel Ring.

    Name: "Turn Back Time" Button
    Effect: Turns back time. Amount traveled back varies by usage. Seems to take target back to significant place. In Ash's case, it takes him back to the courtyard with apple trees where he met you.
    Spell Item: Unknown
    Other Notes: Only spell used by you, a non-mage. Power discovered after meeting Ash. Chronos hailed as the Master of Time. Chronos used a variant on Ash which sent him back to the future

    Name: Umbratas Fleur Magnus: Lancaster
    Effect: Creates a giant lance, used by Princess Riley in battle.
    Spell Item: Unknown

    Name: Fleour Melinor Magnus: Shadow Puppet
    Effect: Using blood from another dead being, a puppet is summoned with the powers of the formerly living entity.
    Spell Item: Unknown
    Other Notes: One of Elise Maybelle's signature spells. It is some form of Illusion magic.

    I know I've missed some. Feel free to add.

    I would also like to ask something. Does learning a spell or its stronger variant have any effect on the story? I haven't been able to actually use the spells for Gravitas or Levitas, as well as their variants, for anything useful.
