    List of Spells

    10-24-2016, 02:24 PM
    *This list is a work-in-progress, but if you guys have any missing spells (there are a lot right now), then be sure to let me know!

    Nexutas
    Ash has been working long and hard to reach you. Who would have thought that the key is in an apple?

    Annextas
    A binding spell that creates a magical binding between two entities, whether a person or a physical object. Riley used this spell to bind the pouch to the pocket watch.

    Levitas
    A low level wind spell that comes in handy when Ash needs to reach higher places. When activated, it can lift Ash a number of feet off the ground.

    Levitas Magnus
    A mid level wind spell that can only be obtained by leveling up Levitas. This spell requires no cast time which makes it extremely applicable in critical moments when the caster needs to make something fly away.

    Levitas Magnus: Force Pulse
    A high level wind spell that sends a pulse of compressed gravity at approximately 900 meters per second making this spell incredibly hard to dodge even for the fastest of targets. Requires no cast time. True Levitas Magnus must be achieved in order to obtain this spell as well as the ability to enter [a] high-level trance lasting up to seven days. Years of study are required to achieve this ability and success is not guaranteed. Although, there are a few mages powerful enough to put others under high level trances which allows a shortcut for mages to obtain high level spells without the hard work. First Mage Ezra is one of the few known mages who can do this and, needless to say, many sought him for this ability.

    Gravitas
    Makes Ash heavier. A low level earth spell. When you need to make a hole on the ground think no further than the word Gravitas.

    Gravitas Magnus
    Increase force of gravity on target. A mid level earth spell that can only be acquired by leveling up Gravitas. This spell requires no time to cast which makes it extremely applicable in critical moment when the caster needs something to be more than grounded.

    Incendiatas
    A low level fire spell obtained from the dissipated energy of a bomb. Useful for turning your hand into a torch, to light things on fire and to warm your hand. Just the one hand for now.
